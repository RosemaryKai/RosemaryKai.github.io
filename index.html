Welcome to Buffalo!

The city of good neighbors! Lovers of football, winters that will tear you apart, and sauce soo good that nobody can ever seem to replicate it correctly...

Football: Buffalo Bills

Buffalonians and Football.. while not all of them are like this (I am one who is not myself)... go hand-and -hand with football! The games bring together neighborhoods and communities just to see their favorite team get smoked in the field. I honestly admire the dedication.

Snowy Winters

Buffalo being right on a great lake massively effects the winters we had. Most people despise it but I myself LOVED the winters of Buffalo! Despite this, they can be very dangerous- and if you're caught in a blizzard... you're probably in a lot of danger.

Buffalo Sauce... yum...

BUFFALO SAUCE!!!! IS THE BEST THING TO EVER TOUCH WESTERN NEW YORK. The city had graciously handed out it's beautiful ability to cook to the rest of the world.. only for them to CONSTANTLY GET IT WRONG!!! Nowhere does it like Buffalo.
